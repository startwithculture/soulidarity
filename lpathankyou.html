Dear all of you,

I hope everybody got home safe and healthy!
It was beautiful meeting all of you during those special times and the very difficult circumstances. First of all I would like to thank Braulio again for the courage and vision he had, to put up a Canary music market and the great selection of artists and delegates. I received so much positive energy from the days on the island and a lot of inspiration from the talks to artists, their music and by meeting all of you.

Mostly, I would like to thank Braulio for giving me the opportunity to introduce #SOULIDARITY and for giving it the room it needs to let its message expand beyond restrictions and limitations.

I promised to some of you, to share postings or links you could use during christmas time to help raise awareness of the situation. If you have the opportunity to share it on your professional or private accounts it would be greatly appreciated. Below I add all relevant information and I hope it’s easy for you to understand, if not - you know how to reach me by email or whatsapp .

In case you have even more ideas or opportunities to support #SOULIDARITY I would be very happy! My presentation from LPA market you can watch here.

I hope that this holiday season brings you the joy and happiness that you deserve and above all excitement for the New Year ahead with more live music, concerts and dancing than ever!

Best Wishes, Julia
