1. Applicants and musicians must be 18 years and older.
2. Applicant must be the band manager or main agent (please email us in case you do not have a manager or agent).
3. Application must be made through the Soulidarity online application form.
4. Applicant and any musicians do NOT have to be sick with Covid-19 to apply for the fund.
5. The musician/s applied for must be resident in Africa, applicant residence can be anywhere. (Musicians living outside of Africa will not be considered)
6. Â¾ of the lost concerts for the musician/s applied for must have been booked outside the African continent
7. Applicant and musicians agree to share and promote the campaign within their network and social media throughout the duration of the campaign.
8. Applicant agrees to communicate the campaign to all promoters who had booked the musicians to encourage them to participate in sharing.
9. Applicant and her/his musicians is committed to providing material for the campaign such as pictures, shout outs, videos, and so on. More info: www.soulidarity.org/application.html
10. START WITH CULTURE has the right to refuse applications when they are faulty and not fitting the criteria.
11. Application can only be made per band/group/artist and not by individual band members.
12. Musicians who play in different bands can only submit under one band - please choose the band you had the most shows with.
13. Applicant agrees to provide further documentation upon request for example concert contracts, cancelation confirmations, and contact details of the promoter.
14. All information given by the applicant will be treated 100% confidential and will not be used otherwise. Applicant agrees to the START WITH CULTURE data collection and privacy policies. (See our data protection policies here www.soulidarity.org/privacy.html)
15. The participation does not entitle participants for any right to payments.
16. Applicant has understood that payments can only be made if money is collected.
17. The relief payment amount can be adjusted as a percentage to the collected fund.
18. Participant(s) do agree to not hold any further demands/requests against START WITH CULTURE after receiving the relief fund payment.
19. Participant must provide passport copies of all band members.
20. In avoidance of an unfair distribution towards the artists, the applicant declares to distribute a min of 75% of the received payments to the musicians he/ she applies for. Proof of payment to the artists can be demanded by START WITH CULTURE at any time. In case the participant fails to provide such documents to START WITH CULTURE, START WITH CULTURE can claim back the payment made to the participant. Applicant can also give START WITH CULTURE the banking details for individual musicians.
21. Applicant agrees that all banking charges and payment provider fees will be due to the recipient of the money.
22. Before the payment is made, the applicant is sent a document by email, which he/she must send back signed within 7 days.
23. Applicant has understood and agreed that the fund is NOT a FEE replacement for lost concerts but it is just designed to provide relief to musicians in a time of need and does not need to get paid back to START WITH CULTURE.
24. Non-discrimination statement: START WITH CULTURE is highlighting the fact that the fund does not discriminate in any way in its processes and it covers musicians making music in any genre as long as they fit the set criteria.
25. Deadline for the applications is Monday 25th of June 2020 midnight.  

Munich, 11. May 2020
