ARTIST APPLICATION

Join the movement

Thank you for your interest in the #SOULIDARITY fund. 
The conditions for eligibility are:
1. only managers or agents can submit applications on behalf of a band or artist
2.  the band/artist must be resident in Africa
3. the band/artist must be an international touring act and can confirm lost shows in Europe, America, Oceania or Asia in the period from March to August 2020 due to COVID-19 pandemic.
4. the artist must provide the content for social media and more as listed in our Artist's Guide

#SOULIDARITY ARTIST'S GUIDE

Check List

You are an Artist

Only managers or agents can submit applications on behalf of a band or artist. Self-managed artists can contact us via email and we will manually verify eligibility to apply.

Not sure if you're eligible?

Go through our questionnaire and find out if you can apply for the #SOULIDARITY fund.

01.

Fill out the application form

Can be only done through a manager or agent! 

02.

After filling out the application form

After you filled out the application form, our team will inform you by email about your approval as soon as you provided all required documents by upload or email to application@start-with-culture.org (latest within 7 working days).

03.

Make Sure

To upload or send by email (latest within 7 working days after submitting the form):
Copy of passports of all band members.
Proof of band members: stage set up or technical rider as a proof for the band members listed in the application process.
Proof of confirmed/canceled shows 

03.

Become Active

Read the Artist's Guide and follow the instructions. We are looking forward to receive the requested material as quick as possible!
