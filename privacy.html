1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
We collect information or data when you sign-up for a donation, submit an application via the call for the Soulidarity Fund on our website, or use any of our services or take part in our events where you need to sign up for. This data helps us to operate our business, campaigns, advertise and improve our platforms, existing services, develop new services and to enhance, improve and customize your experiences while interacting with us or our services. We also save your data for the relief payments, issuing donation receipts for tax purposes and of course, and communicating better with you.

1.1. Applicants for the relief fund
When you submit an application via Soulidarity.org - we collect your and your musicians official or personal information such as your company name, first and last names, links to your social media channels, geographic location, email address, contact number, passport information and any other information you have provided us while registering for the relief fund. This information helps for processing your orders and the payout and tax purposes. We also use this information to connect you with other potential businesses for new business development and networking purposes. Stage riders and passport copies when requested to verify your band activities

1.2 Donors for the relief fund
When you donate for the “SOULIDARITY RELIEF FUND” we collect your first name and last name, street, city, and area code and email address if you wish to receive a valid donation receipt. Otherwise we only collect and keep your email address to keep you updated about the “SOULIDARITY” campaign and its activities around it and other of our activities only if you wish to receive our updates and newsletters. We respect when you want to stay anonym and we will not share any information with any third party.

1.3 When you visit any of our websites or use our apps, we request information such as the browser and device you're using, your IP address, your demographic location, the site you came from, what you did, and or the site you visit when you leave us. This information is to track your user behavior and in no way collects your personal information if you are not signing up. For more information, you can refer to our terms and read about our cookie policy.
You will always be the one in control of your personal information. You can contact us to modify, delete any, or all of your personal information.
